Who We Are

Built on Trust.
Driven by Precision.

Founded in Houston, Texas in 2014, Zavian Power Logistics has grown from a regional carrier to a nationwide freight force. We operate one of the most modern, well-maintained fleets in the industry — and we back every load with the accountability that shippers demand.

Whether you need a power-only move to meet a tight drop window, temperature-controlled reefer service for perishables, or a flatbed haul for oversized industrial equipment, our team is built to deliver on time, every time.
